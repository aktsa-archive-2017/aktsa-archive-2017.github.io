About

At the AK chapter of the NC TSA, we strive to be the best. We aren't satisfied until our work is of a caliber that we would actually use and recommend. In all our efforts, we try to produce professional work and to maintain a positive, cooperative learning environment. We know that computers and technology are the future, and we want to be ready to take on all the challenges of our globalized, automated world, in order to become successful 21st Century adults. In all things, we try to follow our motto: "Change Through Action".

Our School - Ardrey Kell

First opened in 2006, Ardrey Kell is, we believe, the greatest high-school in North Carolina. It also has the best CTE department. Out school offers classes in a variety of CTE fields, including Marketing, Graphics, Business Education, Drafting, and more.
